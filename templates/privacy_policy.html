Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 30, 2025

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and business information when you contact us, subscribe to newsletters, or request services.
  • Project Information: Business details, project requirements, budget information, and communication preferences when you submit intake forms or engage our services.
  • Usage Data: IP address, browser type, device information, pages visited, time spent on pages, and referral sources through Google Analytics and other tracking technologies.
  • Cookies and Tracking: Essential cookies for site functionality, analytics cookies for performance measurement, and preference cookies (e.g., cookie consent settings).
  • Communication Records: Email correspondence, consultation notes, and support interactions for service delivery and record-keeping.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Service Delivery: To provide web design services, communicate about projects, and fulfill contractual obligations
  • Communication: To respond to inquiries, send newsletters (only with consent), and provide customer support
  • Business Operations: To process payments, maintain business records, and manage client relationships
  • Website Improvement: To analyze site performance, user behavior, and optimize user experience
  • Marketing: To promote our services to interested prospects (only with proper consent and opt-out options)
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legitimate business interests

3. Sharing Your Information

We never sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share your information in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party services (hosting providers, email services, payment processors) who assist in business operations under strict confidentiality agreements
  • Analytics: Anonymized usage data with Google Analytics and similar services for website performance analysis
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or to protect our legal rights and safety
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a business sale or merger (with prior notice to affected users)
  • Consent: With your explicit consent for specific purposes

4. Your Rights

Under applicable data protection laws, you have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate personal data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured format
  • Withdrawal of Consent: Unsubscribe from communications anytime
  • Object to Processing: Object to certain uses of your data

5. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data based on:

  • Consent: When you subscribe to newsletters or contact us
  • Legitimate Interest: For website analytics and improvement
  • Contract Performance: To provide requested services

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy:

  • Contact and intake inquiries: 3 years for business records and potential follow-up
  • Active client projects: Duration of project plus 7 years for business and tax records
  • Newsletter subscriptions: Until you unsubscribe or account becomes inactive (2+ years no engagement)
  • Website analytics data: 26 months (Google Analytics standard retention period)
  • Email communications: 3 years for business correspondence records
  • Payment records: 7 years as required by tax and business law

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. This includes encryption, secure server hosting, and regular security updates.

8. Contact Us

For any privacy-related questions, concerns, or to exercise your rights, please contact us:

  • Email: krystamcalister@thegreycanvas.co
  • Subject Line: "Privacy Policy Inquiry"
  • Response Time: We will respond within 30 days

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we make significant changes, we will notify you by email (if you have provided your email address) or by posting a notice on our website. The updated policy will be effective from the date specified in the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy.
