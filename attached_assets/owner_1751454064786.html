About

The Owner: Krysta McAlister

Krysta McAlister is the resilient heart behind The Grey Canvas. A freelance web designer based in DFW, Krysta brings not just skill, but grit, empathy, and an unshakable work ethic shaped by real-life challenges. As a single mom of three with a background in education, Krysta transitioned into tech after teaching herself to code—motivated by a desire to build a better future for her kids and honor the legacy of her late father, who was her greatest support.

After earning her Meta Back-End Developer certification and an AAS in Software Development from Collin College, she began designing custom websites that empower other small business owners—especially those in construction, real estate, trades, and freelance services—to take control of their online presence.

Krysta's journey from paraprofessional to junior developer in Dallas proves that skill can be learned—but passion and perseverance can’t be taught. Her previous role as Director of Digital and Business Development at K.W. Plumbing in Argyle, TX, gave her hands-on experience building an entire digital infrastructure from scratch. She knows the grind of launching a business and uses that understanding to deliver websites that are not only beautiful and mobile-friendly but also deeply functional, search-optimized, and easy to manage.

Whether you need an affordable web design in Denton TX, an e-commerce developer for a small business, or someone who understands how to build websites for electricians, real estate agents, or freelancers, Krysta’s lived experience and technical expertise make her the ideal partner.

The Grey Canvas is more than a business—it’s a mission to show her kids that no obstacle is too big, and that a mom with a dream (and a laptop) can change the game.
