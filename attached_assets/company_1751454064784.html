About The Grey Canvas

Web. Design. Thrive.

The Grey Canvas is a solo web design and development studio based in the Dallas/Fort Worth area, run by Krysta McAlister. Specializing in affordable web design for Dallas TX and beyond, Krysta blends aesthetics and functionality to craft powerful websites for service-based small businesses in Texas—especially those in construction, real estate, design, and skilled trades.

As a freelance web designer in DFW, Krysta builds custom sites that are scalable, mobile-friendly, SEO-ready, and easy for you to manage. Whether you're a contractor needing a professional online presence, a freelancer ready to showcase your services, or a startup in Fort Worth looking to grow, The Grey Canvas is here to help you thrive online.

Need an e-commerce developer for a small business or someone who understands the unique needs of electricians, mechanics, and real estate agents? Krysta tailors every project to your goals, industry, and growth potential. It’s not just about having a website—it’s about building a digital foundation that works as hard as you do.
